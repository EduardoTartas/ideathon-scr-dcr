# SCR-DCR

## Product Purpose
SCR-DCR is a municipal platform that converts proper waste disposal into tax credits (IPTU and waste fees). It builds a transparent, end-to-end flow from disposal to fiscal abatements, reducing irregular dumping and improving recycling rates.

## Goals
- Environmental: reduce improper disposal in streets, vacant lots, and rivers.
- Social: engage citizens and the construction sector with tangible incentives.
- Economic: decrease municipal cleaning and waste-handling costs.
- Technological: provide traceability from disposal to tax credit redemption.

## Primary Users
- Citizen or company: wants clear credit balance, nearest PEV, and simple redemption.
- Collection point operator: needs a fast, low-friction disposal registration flow.
- Municipal manager: needs KPIs, auditability, and conversion rule control.

## First-View Priorities
- Citizen: current credits, equivalent value in BRL, and nearby PEVs.
- Operator: disposal registration form with immediate feedback.
- Manager: monthly KPIs, collected volume, issued credits, estimated savings.

## Voice and Tone
Accessible, civic, and practical. Focus on clarity and trust.

## Anti-References
Avoid a generic SaaS look. Avoid overly bureaucratic government layouts.
